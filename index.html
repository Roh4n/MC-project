Welcome to FoodTalk's Page.

This app allows user to talk to strangers in vicnity while sitting in a restaurant. The main purpose of the app is to ask for a rating & quality of a particular food item from the fellow diners at a restaurant.Let it be any occasion say,you want to try a new place or a new dish or just want to talk to someone. This app is definitely for you.Happy eating! Happy talking!
